"Baa, Baa, Black Sheep"

Baa, baa, black sheep, have you any wool?
Yes, sir, yes, sir, three bags full!
One for the master, one for the dame,
And one for the little boy who lives down the lane.
Baa, baa, black sheep, what do you say?
I share my warmth in a cozy way.
With blankets and sweaters, soft and bright,
Keeping everyone snug through the cold night.
